Overview

Resident Evil 4 Remake is a modern reimagining of the iconic 2005 survival horror game. Developed by Capcom, the remake retains the core storyline and characters while introducing updated graphics, enhanced gameplay mechanics, and a darker tone to match modern expectations.
The game was released on March 24, 2023. It launched on Playstation 4, Playstation 5, Xbox Series S/X, and PC. On December 20 of the same year, a mobile version on Apple's latest Iphone was released alongside a version for Mac.
The game scored incredibly positive reviews and sold over 20 million copies earning its rightful place alongside the legendary original Resident Evil 4 that launched in 2005.
